<p><ul><li>Air-cooled Motorized Pulley</li><li>Oil-cooled Motorized Pulley</li><li>Oil-immersed Motorized Pulley</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorized pulley market is categorized into three main types: air-cooled, oil-cooled, and oil-immersed motorized pulleys. Air-cooled motorized pulleys utilize ambient air for cooling, making them suitable for light-duty applications. Oil-cooled motorized pulleys use circulation of oil for efficient thermal management, enhancing performance and longevity, ideal for medium to heavy-duty uses. Oil-immersed motorized pulleys are fully submerged in oil, providing superior cooling and protection, best suited for high-load and demanding industrial environments. Each type offers distinct advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
