<p><strong>The Residential Connected Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WiFi</li><li>ZigBee</li><li>Z-Wave</li><li>Bluetooth</li><li>Thread</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential connected lighting encompasses various communication technologies that enable smart lighting systems. WiFi allows high-speed internet connectivity with ample range but can be affected by network congestion. ZigBee is a low-power, mesh networking standard ideal for connecting multiple devices in homes. Z-Wave focuses on low-energy, reliable communication over short distances, suitable for smart home setups. Bluetooth facilitates direct device pairing within a limited range, while Thread provides a secure, low-latency mesh connectivity option, enhancing interoperability among smart devices.</p></p>
