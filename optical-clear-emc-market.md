<p><p>The Optical Clear Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC) market is divided into two main types: solid and liquid Optical Clear EMC. In contrast, Liquid Optical Clear EMC consists of coatings or gels that can be applied to surfaces, offering flexibility and ease of application, particularly in complex shapes or large areas, while maintaining optical clarity and EMC performance.</p></p>
