<p><ul><li>Boiled Bone Meal</li><li>Rough Bone Meal</li><li>Steamed Bone Meal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone meal market includes three primary types: boiled bone meal, rough bone meal, and steamed bone meal. Boiled bone meal involves a process where bones are cooked under pressure, making them more digestible and nutrient-rich. Rough bone meal is coarsely ground and retains larger particles, suitable for specific agricultural uses. Steamed bone meal is produced by steaming bones to eliminate impurities, resulting in a fine powder that is often used in fertilizers due to its high phosphorus content and essential nutrients for plant growth.</p></p>
