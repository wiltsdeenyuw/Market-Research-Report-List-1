<p><p>The academic software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted online, allowing easy access for users from any location and enabling seamless collaboration and updates. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on institutional servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. Institutions choose between these models based on their needs for flexibility, control, and resource availability in managing academic processes.</p></p>
