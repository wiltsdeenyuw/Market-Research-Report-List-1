<p><strong>The Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Method</li><li>Dry Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen (CRMB) market encompasses two primary methods: the wet method and the dry method. The wet method involves blending rubber crumbs with heated bitumen, resulting in a homogenous mixture that enhances performance characteristics such as viscosity and flexibility. Conversely, the dry method incorporates rubber into the aggregate before mixing with asphalt, allowing for a more uniform distribution. Both methods aim to improve road durability, resistance to aging, and overall performance, catering to varying project requirements in the asphalt industry.</p></p>
