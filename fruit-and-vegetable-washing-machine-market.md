<p><p>The Fruit and Vegetable Washing Machine market features two primary types: Ultrasonic and Ozone. Ultrasonic machines use high-frequency sound waves to create microscopic bubbles in water, effectively removing dirt and pesticides without damaging produce. Ozone machines utilize ozone gas to disinfect and purify fruits and vegetables, ensuring a thorough cleanse while reducing chemical residues. Both types focus on enhancing food safety and extending shelf life by effectively removing contaminants while preserving the quality of the produce.</p></p>
