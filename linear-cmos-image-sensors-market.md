<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Linear CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Side Illuminated Linear CMOS Image Sensors</li><li>Back Side Illuminated Linear CMOS Image Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear CMOS image sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Front Side Illuminated (FSI) and Back Side Illuminated (BSI) sensors. FSI sensors capture light from the front side, making them simpler and cost-effective but less sensitive to low light. BSI sensors, on the other hand, capture light from the back, allowing for improved sensitivity and higher image quality in low-light conditions. Together, these technologies serve diverse applications, from machine vision to medical imaging.</p></p>
