<p><p>Continuous level measurement transmitters are essential for monitoring liquid and solid levels in various industries. Differential pressure transmitters use pressure variations to gauge level changes. Guided wave radar utilizes microwave pulses along a probe for precise measurements. Non-contact radar measures level using radar waves without physical contact. Ultrasonic transmitters emit sound waves to determine levels based on reflection time. Capacitance technology measures changes in capacitance caused by level variations. Other types may include laser and floats, catering to specific applications.</p></p>
