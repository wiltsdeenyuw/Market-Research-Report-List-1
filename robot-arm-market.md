<p><p>The robot arm market is primarily categorized into traditional robot arms and collaborative robot arms. Traditional robot arms are designed for high-speed and precision tasks in industrial settings, typically operating in isolation due to safety concerns. In contrast, collaborative robot arms, or cobots, prioritize human interaction and safety, allowing them to work alongside humans in shared environments. This flexibility makes cobots suitable for a variety of applications, especially in smaller businesses, while traditional arms dominate larger manufacturing processes.</p></p>
