<p><p>The blow moulding tools market consists of three primary types: extrusion, injection, and stretch blow moulding. Extrusion blow moulding involves forming a tube of molten plastic that is expanded into a mould to create a hollow part. Injection blow moulding combines injection moulding for preforms and blow moulding for shaping, offering high precision. Stretch blow moulding stretches the plastic preform into the mould, enhancing clarity and strength, suitable for producing lightweight bottles and containers. Each method serves distinct manufacturing needs.</p></p>
