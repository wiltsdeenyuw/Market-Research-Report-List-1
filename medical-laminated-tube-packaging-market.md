<p><strong>The Medical Laminated Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical laminated tube packaging primarily includes two types: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminates) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminates). ABL tubes combine aluminum layers with plastic to provide superior protection against moisture, light, and oxygen, making them ideal for sensitive pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. In contrast, PBL tubes consist of various plastic layers, offering flexibility and design versatility, while still providing adequate barrier properties. Both types ensure product integrity, enhance shelf life, and improve user convenience in the medical and personal care sectors.</p></p>
