<p><p>The Heavy Commercial Vehicle HVAC market is categorized into two primary types: engine-powered HVAC and electric-powered HVAC systems. In contrast, electric-powered HVAC systems leverage electricity, either from batteries or external sources, providing a quieter and more environmentally friendly alternative. Both systems are designed to enhance passenger comfort and operational efficiency in heavy commercial vehicles under varied climatic conditions.</p></p>
