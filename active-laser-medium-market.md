<p><p>The active laser medium market includes four main types: solid, liquid, gaseous, and plasma. Solid lasers can be crystalline or amorphous and utilize solid-state materials for light amplification, known for durability and efficient energy conversion. Liquid lasers use dye solutions or other liquids, providing tunability and versatility. Gaseous lasers employ gas as the active medium, often offering high power and beam quality. Plasma lasers utilize ionized gases, capable of generating high-intensity beams and suitable for advanced applications.</p></p>
