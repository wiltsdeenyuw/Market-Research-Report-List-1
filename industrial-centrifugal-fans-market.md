<p><strong>The Industrial Centrifugal Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forward</li><li>Backward</li><li>Radial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial centrifugal fans are categorized into three main types based on their blade design and airflow characteristics. Forward fans have blades that curve in the direction of rotation, providing high airflow at lower pressures, making them suitable for applications needing high volume. Backward fans, with blades angled backwards, are designed for higher efficiency and better performance under varying loads, ideal for higher pressure applications. Radial fans feature straight blades, delivering a balanced airflow and pressure, commonly used in dust collection and extraction systems.</p></p>
