<p><ul><li>Gas Absorption Tower</li><li>Liquid Absorption Tower</li><li>Gas and Liquid Absorption Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absorption tower market is categorized into three main types based on the phase involved in the absorption process. Gas absorption towers extract specific gases from a gas mixture using a liquid absorbent, primarily in air pollution control and chemical processing. Liquid absorption towers involve the absorption of soluble substances in liquid media, often utilized in wastewater treatment. Gas and liquid absorption towers combine both phases, effectively capturing and removing components from gaseous and liquid mixtures, facilitating diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
