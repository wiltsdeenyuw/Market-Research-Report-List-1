<p><strong>The Infrared Thermopile Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensor</li><li>SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared thermopile temperature sensor market comprises two primary types: through hole thermopile sensors and surface mount device (SMD) thermopile sensors. Through hole sensors are designed for traditional mounting on printed circuit boards (PCBs) with lead connections, offering robust performance in various applications. In contrast, SMD thermopile sensors provide a compact form factor, facilitating automated assembly and minimizing space on PCBs. Both types are widely utilized in applications like HVAC systems, medical devices, and industrial monitoring for non-contact temperature measurements.</p></p>
