<p><strong>The Train Signalling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Block ATC System</li><li>Moving Block ATC System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The train signalling system market comprises two primary types: Fixed Block ATC and Moving Block ATC systems. Fixed Block ATC relies on predetermined sections of track or "blocks" to control train movements, enhancing safety and minimizing collisions. In contrast, Moving Block ATC allows for dynamic spacing between trains, utilizing real-time data for more efficient use of track capacity, reducing delays, and enabling higher speeds. Both systems are essential for modern railway operations, ensuring safe and efficient transit.</p></p>
