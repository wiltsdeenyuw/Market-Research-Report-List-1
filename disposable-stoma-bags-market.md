<p><p>The disposable stoma bags market encompasses two main types: one-piece and two-piece bags. One-piece bags combine the pouch and barrier into a single unit, offering convenience and ease of use, ideal for active individuals. Conversely, two-piece bags feature a separate pouch and skin barrier, allowing users to change the pouch without removing the barrier, enhancing customization and comfort. Both types cater to diverse patient needs, emphasizing skin health, discretion, and lifestyle adaptability in post-surgical care.</p></p>
