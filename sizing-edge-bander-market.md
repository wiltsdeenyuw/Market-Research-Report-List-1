<p><p>The sizing edge bander market is primarily divided into two types: single-side and double-side edge banders. Single-side edge banders are designed to apply edge banding to one side of a panel, making them ideal for simpler projects or smaller workshops. Conversely, double-side edge banders can apply banding to both sides in one pass, enhancing efficiency and productivity for larger operations. Both types cater to varying production needs, with double-side edge banders generally preferred for high-volume manufacturing.</p></p>
