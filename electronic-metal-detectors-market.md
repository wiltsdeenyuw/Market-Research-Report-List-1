<p><strong>The Electronic Metal Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic metal detectors market includes three main types: Very Low Frequency (VLF), Pulse Induction (PI), and Beat-Frequency Oscillation (BFO). VLF detectors use two coils to identify metal types and depths, offering high sensitivity for small items. Pulse Induction detectors send short bursts of energy, making them effective in highly mineralized soils and for deep targets. BFO detectors utilize two oscillating frequencies to detect metals, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness, suitable for various recreational and professional applications.</p></p>
