<p><ul><li>Analog Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Pulse Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoelectric effect sensor market includes two primary types: analog photoelectric sensors and pulse photoelectric sensors. Analog photoelectric sensors provide continuous output that varies with the intensity of light detected, making them suitable for applications requiring fine sensitivity. In contrast, pulse photoelectric sensors operate by emitting light pulses and detecting reflected signals, which are used for precise distance measurement and object detection. Both types play critical roles in automation, robotics, and sensing applications across various industries.</p></p>
