<p><p>Barge logistics transportation encompasses the movement of goods via barges, classified primarily into two types: dry cargo and liquid cargo barges. Dry cargo barges are designed for transporting bulk items like coal, grain, or construction materials, emphasizing durability and capacity. In contrast, liquid cargo barges are tailored for transporting liquids such as chemicals, petroleum, or water, featuring specialized containment systems to prevent spills and ensure safety. Both types play a significant role in efficient, cost-effective inland shipping and logistics.</p></p>
