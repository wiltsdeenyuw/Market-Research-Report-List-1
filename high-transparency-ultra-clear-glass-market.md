<p><p>The High Transparency Ultra Clear Glass market encompasses distinct types, primarily rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, resulting in a smooth surface and varying thickness, ideal for architectural applications. In contrast, float glass is formed by floating molten glass on molten tin, creating a uniform thickness and high optical clarity, making it suitable for windows and displays. Both types are valued for their excellent light transmission and aesthetic appeal in various industries.</p></p>
