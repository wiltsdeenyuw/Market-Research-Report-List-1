<p><p>Ball screws are critical components in machine tools, transforming rotary motion into linear motion with high precision. The market for ball screws includes two primary types: internal circulation ball screws and external circulation ball screws. Internal circulation ball screws feature a closed-loop design, allowing for a compact structure, ideal for applications with space constraints. Conversely, external circulation ball screws have balls that travel in a visible path outside the nut, providing enhanced load capacity and efficiency, suitable for larger-scale applications.</p></p>
