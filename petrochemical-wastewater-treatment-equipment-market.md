<p><p>The petrochemical wastewater treatment equipment market comprises several key types. Biological treatment utilizes microorganisms to degrade organic pollutants. Ion exchange treatment involves the removal of contaminants by exchanging unwanted ions with less harmful ions. Membrane treatment employs filters to separate solids and pollutants from water, ensuring high purity. Physico-chemical treatment combines physical and chemical processes to remove contaminants, often through coagulation or sedimentation. Each method is tailored to effectively address specific wastewater challenges within the petrochemical industry.</p></p>
